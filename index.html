About Me

I am a passionate AI/ML engineer and mathematician with over 15 years of experience in computer vision, optimization, and scientific computing. My work blends mathematical rigor with practical machine learning, from medical image diagnostics to generative models and cybersecurity systems.

  • Specialties: Image Processing, NLP, Generative AI, Scientific ML, Optimization

Projects

  • WeCAViT – Pneumonia Detection in Chest X-Rays: Developed a weighted CNN model for robust and interpretable diagnosis of pneumonia from medical images. Improved feature extraction for low-contrast areas using customized CNN weighting strategies.
  • Detection of AI-Generated Images: Designed a novel method combining texture and frequency analysis at the patch level to detect synthetic visual content generated by generative models. Applied it to benchmark datasets for AI image verification.
  • Cybersecurity Threat Detection System: Engineered an NLP-powered ML pipeline to identify phishing URLs and malicious email content in real time. Used HuggingFace transformers, feature engineering, and XGBoost, deployed on AWS with integrated threat response.
